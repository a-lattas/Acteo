About Acteo

Αcteo is a word whose etymology derives from the ancient greek word akteos (ἀκταῖος) which means coastal.

Acteo is your coastal partner on your maritime activity and facilitates crew hiring and selection for voyages. From now on, thanks to Acteo, maritime recruitment process is based on profile selection rendering the route and crew scheduling faster and easier.

Services

Acteo offers added value to both crew candidates and companies:

Crew candidates

It is a common fact that maritime crew often awaits for a long period after a voyage. However, that is not always a choice. Acteo helps crew candidates find a voyage as quick as possible by displaying all the inscripted companies and the positions demanded by them.

Companies

Recruiting was never an easy case for the shipping and maritime industries. In this context, our platform empowers recruiters with a great pool of candidates so as to keep the route schedule on time by hiring the best crew available.
